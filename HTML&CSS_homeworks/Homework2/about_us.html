About us

The Face and Body Shop is the first online store driven by the nature. This means that in our store we only sell products that are 100% vegan and cruelty free.

By taking care about the nature, you take care about yourself!

The company was founded by a female individual struggling to find the best products for the skin considering the condition she had on her face. The initial idea came up when the founder started experimenting with natural ingredients and noticed that the skin on her face and body inmproved significantly.

This led her to begin researching and analyzing ingredients in the products, looking for a scheme or pattern and comapring ratings between various trusted sources.

After a few months of resarching and experimenting, she started her first collaboration with a skincare brand. After the first collaboration followed many more collaborations with very well-known natural and organic skincare brands.

Today The Face and Body shop offers a variety of products and brands, and there are many more to come in the future.
